Description

The purpose of this study is to help us improve on the design of our website. This task requires that you install a plug-in to proceed. The plugin will allow you to share your screen and microphone for the duration of the test. For this task, it is important to “think out loud.” For example, pretend we are having a conversation: If I asked you, “how many windows are in your home?”, how would you respond? Instructions

Please note that for this test, you’ll be using a prototype, not a finished product. Some features may be missing or unresponsive. If something doesn’t work as expected, tell us what you think should happen.

  1. Look around this screen, please tell us what you think you can do on this site.
  2. Show and tell us how you would browse for an accessory.
  3. Show and tell us what steps you would take to purchase an item.

Note to participants: Please leave this screen open while participating in the test.
